## How to Use

### Viewing the Brochure

1. Click the **Download Brochure** button above
2. The brochure will open in a new browser tab
3. You can read it directly in your browser

### Creating a PDF

To save or share the brochure as a PDF:

1. Open the brochure in your browser (click button above)
2. Use your browser's Print function:
   - **Chrome/Edge:** Press `Cmd+P` (Mac) or `Ctrl+P` (Windows)
   - **Firefox:** Press `Cmd+P` (Mac) or `Ctrl+P` (Windows)
   - **Safari:** Press `Cmd+P` (Mac)
3. In the print dialog:
   - Select **"Save as PDF"** as the destination
   - **Important:** Enable **"Background graphics"** option for best results
   - Keep scale at 100%
4. Click **Save** and choose your location

**Recommended:** Use **Chrome** or **Edge** for the best PDF export quality.
